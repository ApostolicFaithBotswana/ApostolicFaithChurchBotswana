Recent Events

A Year of God's Grace

Throughout 2026, God has faithfully blessed the Apostolic Faith Church Botswana through conventions, conferences, youth camps, worship, fellowship, and lives transformed by His grace. This newsletter highlights some of the major events that shaped our year.

Easter Convention

Christ Our Passover

The 2026 Easter Convention united believers from Francistown, Letlhakane, Mmutlane, and Gaborone under the theme "Christ Our Passover" (1 Corinthians 5:7).

Through Bible studies, sermons, youth services, testimonies, devotional meetings, and musical concerts, participants reflected on Christ's sacrifice, resurrection, and the hope of salvation. The convention strengthened fellowship across Botswana while encouraging believers to deepen their faith and boldly share the Gospel.

Easter 2026
Mother's Conference

My Beauty, My Pride, I Am God's Masterpiece

Held from 8–10 May 2026, this inspiring conference celebrated women through worship, biblical teaching, testimonies, and practical workshops. Ladies were encouraged to embrace their identity in Christ while growing spiritually, emotionally, physically, and financially.

Sessions on health, stewardship, healing, and personal development equipped participants to serve God, their families, and their communities with renewed confidence and purpose.

Mother's Conference · 2026
Youth Camp

Soar High 2026

Centred on the theme "SOAR HIGH", our 2026 YOUTH CAMP brought together young people for four days of worship, Bible teaching, prayer, fellowship, and personal reflection.

Powerful messages, concerts, discussions, and testimonies inspired participants to discover their identity in Christ, live with purpose, and shine His light in their communities. Many experienced spiritual renewal and left with strengthened faith and lasting friendships.
