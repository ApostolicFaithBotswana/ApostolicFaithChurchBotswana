Who We Are

Sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ

Welcome to the Apostolic Faith Church Botswana, where we are committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and helping people develop a personal relationship with God. As part of the worldwide Apostolic Faith Church headquartered in Portland, Oregon, we uphold the fundamental teachings of New Testament faith and believe the Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God.

Across Botswana, our churches provide a place of worship, prayer, biblical teaching, Christian fellowship, and spiritual growth. Whether you are seeking God for the first time or looking for a church family, you are warmly welcome to worship with us.

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ through prayer, preaching, and sharing His message with our communities.

Our Beliefs

We believe the Bible is God's infallible Word and uphold the fundamental teachings of New Testament faith, including salvation, sanctification, and the baptism of the Holy Spirit.

Our Community

We are a welcoming family of believers across Botswana, committed to worship, prayer, Christian fellowship, and living holy lives that glorify God.
